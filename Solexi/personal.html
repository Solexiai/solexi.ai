Recipes & Culinary Heritage

The dishes that span generations deserve to be preserved.

How does it work?

Step 1: Preservation

Record Grandma Lise's complete recipe: ingredients, detailed steps, secret tips, and even a video of her preparing it. Add photos, handwritten notes, the story behind the dish.

Step 2: Security

Your recipe is stored with military-grade encryption in our digital vault. It is backed up in multiple secure datacenters around the world for 100-year guaranteed preservation.

Step 3: Heirs Designation

Decide who will have access to this recipe: your children, grandchildren, or the whole family. You can even schedule automatic transmission at their 18th birthday, their wedding, or any specific moment.

Step 4: Transmission Through Time

In 20, 50 or 100 years, your descendants will be able to discover the apple pie exactly as Grandma Lise made it. With her voice explaining, her gestures filmed, her secrets revealed.

Real Example

"Marie is 68 years old. She records her famous apple pie - the one her mother taught her in 1965. She films the complete preparation, explains each step, tells the story of this recipe that came from Alsace with her great-grandmother in 1920.

She designates her 3 children and her 7 grandchildren as heirs. She schedules the transmission: her children have immediate access, but her grandchildren will only receive it at age 18.

In 2045, her great-granddaughter Emma, who wasn't born yet, receives a notification: "You have received an inheritance from your great-grandmother Marie". She discovers the video, hears her voice, learns the recipe, and perpetuates the family tradition."

How does the Heir Keys system work?

The principle in 4 simple steps

1
Heirs Designation

You choose who will receive your content: children, grandchildren, spouse, close friends. You can designate as many heirs as you wish.

2
Access Conditions

Define WHEN they will receive access: after your death, at a specific date, at age 18, at their wedding, or according to any other condition you establish.

3
Secure Verification

Our system automatically verifies conditions. With death certificate, notary validation, or simple calendar according to your settings. Two-factor authentication for maximum security.

4
Automatic Transmission

Your heirs receive a secure notification. They access your content with their own unique Heir Key, impossible to duplicate or hack.

Heir Key

A secure system that ensures your content will only be accessible to the people you have designated, at the chosen time.

  • Multiple heir designation (unlimited)
  • Customizable access conditions (date, age, event)
  • Automatic notification via secure email/SMS
  • Mandatory two-factor authentication
  • Revocable and modifiable at any time
  • Complete and traceable access log
How it works:
Each heir receives a unique Heir Key (cryptographic code impossible to duplicate). This key only activates once all conditions are met. You maintain full control and can modify the parameters at any time.

Testamentary Document

Store your legal and testamentary documents securely with restricted access and certified timestamping.

  • Secure legal storage (AES-256 encryption)
  • Certified timestamping with blockchain
  • Legal compliance (notary validation possible)
  • Qualified electronic signature
  • Multi-notary access if necessary
  • Guaranteed preservation for 100 years minimum
Legal value:
Your documents are timestamped and certified according to eIDAS standards. They have evidentiary value in court. Possibility of co-signature with your notary to strengthen legal validity.
