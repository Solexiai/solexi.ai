General Questions

What is Solexi.ai and how does it work?

Solexi.ai is an AI-powered digital legacy preservation platform that helps families preserve memories, build family trees, and create voice avatars for future generations.

The process works in 5 steps:

  1. Upload Memories: Photos, videos, documents to a secure encrypted vault
  2. Build Family Tree: Unlimited generations and relationships
  3. Create AI Avatar: Record your voice and stories
  4. Set Transmission: Define when and how heirs access your legacy
  5. Preserve Forever: 100+ years with military-grade encryption

How is Solexi.ai different from Ancestry.com or FamilySearch?

Solexi.ai focuses on preserving modern digital legacy with AI technology, while Ancestry.com and FamilySearch specialize in historical genealogy research.
