The System

Three core mechanisms reduce future ambiguity, preserve user intent, and improve clarity for executors and beneficiaries.

Structured Vault

Records are organized with clear structure and governance so heirs are not forced to reconstruct intent from scattered files, accounts, and disconnected instructions.

Controlled Access Architecture

Permissions are role-based and intent-driven. Access is scoped by purpose and constrained with least-privilege logic to reduce unnecessary exposure.

Verified Delivery Framework

Delivery workflows combine time triggers, multi-step verification, inactivity detection, and controlled execution paths before release actions are completed.

Why Structured Access Matters

  • Digital silence creates friction when operational context is missing.
  • Executors face complexity across systems, credentials, and responsibilities.
  • Intent without structure creates ambiguity at moments that require precision.

Solexi is designed as preventive digital infrastructure.

FAQ

Is this digital immortality?

No. Solexi focuses on structured digital continuity and controlled information transfer.

Who controls access?

You control and update access policy with role-based permissions aligned to your intent.

What if I change beneficiaries?

You can update beneficiaries and access instructions as your family or legal context changes.

Can I export everything?

Data export and portability are part of the platform design objective.

What happens if Solexi evolves?

The roadmap is continuity-focused, with conservative infrastructure decisions and portable data principles.
