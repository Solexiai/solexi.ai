Frequently asked questions about the process

How long does complete registration take?

Basic registration takes 2 minutes. To fully configure your account with your first content and heirs, allow about 30-45 minutes. But you can do it gradually, no need to do everything at once.

Is there a storage limit?

No, absolutely no limit! Whether you have 10 GB or 10 TB of content, you can store everything. We never charge based on space used, only on the features you need.

Can I modify my choices after configuration?

Yes, absolutely! You can at any time add/remove heirs, modify transmission dates, add new content, change your settings. Nothing is fixed, you maintain total control.

What happens if I no longer pay my subscription?

Your content remains saved for 2 years without any action required. You can reactivate at any time. If you pass away, transmission to heirs happens automatically, even without an active subscription (it's in our DNA to respect your final wishes).

How will my heirs know they have an inheritance?

When transmission conditions are met, we contact them by email, SMS and registered mail. They receive their unique Heir Key. Even if they've never heard of Solexi, our clear instructions guide them step by step.
